We offer all follow-up checks as well as device checks directly and without complications in our Heart Centre.

Implanted technical devices must be checked regularly to ensure that they are functioning properly. For this purpose, our software is connected to the implanted device via radio, its functionality is ensured and the setting is adjusted if necessary.
